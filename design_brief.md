# UI/UX Design Brief
**Document Class: Design Specification**

---

## 1. Visual Identity & Mood
* **Overall Feel**: Modern, trustworthy, premium, and user-friendly.
* **Theme**: Deep dark interface with vibrant purple, cyan, and green accents.

---

## 2. Color Palette & Design Tokens

### Core Colors
* **Primary Violet**: `#7C5CFC` (Main action brand color)
* **Secondary Cyan**: `#22D3EE` (Accent and link highlights)
* **Success Green**: `#10B891` (Success alerts, active states, progress indicators)
* **Warning Gold**: `#F59E0B` (Warning indicators and metrics status)
* **Danger Red**: `#EF4444` (Error states, alerts)

### Backgrounds & Borders
* **Global Background**: `#0F172A` (Slate 900)
* **Card Surface**: `#1E293B` (Slate 800)
* **Border Color**: `#334155` (Slate 700)
* **Light Text (Primary)**: `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50)
* **Muted Text (Secondary)**: `#CBD5E1` (Slate 300)

---

## 3. Typography
* **Primary Font**: **Poppins**
* **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
* **Design Execution**: Smooth line-height scaling with distinct hierarchy.

---

## 4. UI Components Guidelines

### Buttons
* **Primary Button**: Solid `#7C5CFC` fill, white text, clean rounded-lg borders with hover elevation.
* **Secondary Button**: Outline with `#334155` border, light-gray text, transition states.

### Cards
* Glass-like container utilizing `#1E293B` backing and `#334155` borders.
* Top margins or side color markers using Primary Purple or Success Green depending on item classification.

### Alerts
* **Success Alert**: Green background (`#10B891`/10%), green border, checkmark icon.
* **Warning Alert**: Orange background (`#F59E0B`/10%), orange border, triangle alert icon.
* **Danger Alert**: Red background (`#EF4444`/10%), red border, error cross icon.
